HORIZON


Horizon is the most voluminous and the largest annual cultural youth inter-college festival conceptualized, designed, organized and executed by Rotaract Club of Nirma Institutes. Held towards the end of February or the start of March every year, this national level annual extravaganza aims at encouraging people to unleash their innovative potential and create an unforgettable experience for each and every visitor.

From singing to dancing, dramatics to quizzing and literary to sports, Horizon embraces more than 55 competitions of various genres revolving around the theme. Spliced with a fashion show, a talent hunt and an Informals section along with phenomenal and gripping performances by the biggest stars of India, RCNI provides a three day spectacle of unparalleled excitement and nerve wrecking competitions.

RCNI created a revolution with Horizon’06 and since then it has never looked back. With the indigenous efforts of a proficient team Horizon has been getting bigger and better with every passing year. Horizon’11 saw AGNEE band’s electrifying performance followed by SUNIDHI CHAUHAN's Live-in Concert and DJ RINK night during Horizon’12 and then taking a huge leap ahead, Horizon’13 witnessed MOHIT CHAUHAN Live-In Concert, DJ LIL’B night and performances by BHOOMI TRIVEDI, KAMLESH PATEL and AISHWARYA MAJUMDAR, breaking all past records with a crowd of 10000+ people! Further last year, RCNI had invited the DID fame MANGESH MONDAL, Indian Idol finalist NIRVESH DAVE and Shabaash India superstar SUNIL SHARMA to judge its events. Moreover, in association with Rotary International Dist. 3051, Live-In-Concert by VISHAL and SHEKHAR was hosted by RCNI in celebration of polio free India. This year, RCNI made history by inviting the winners of India’s Dancing Superstar- The MJ5 Group to one of its auditorium events.

Come March 2015, embark on this journey as the gates will open to a fiesta filled with unparalleled excitement, competitions and spot events, workshops conducted by various professional artists, and the incredible performances from the biggest stars of the Indian entertainment industry.
